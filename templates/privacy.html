Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, email address, and message content when you contact us.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about which papers and features are accessed.
  • Session data: Temporary session information to remember your preferences (e.g., dark mode toggle).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Maintain the security and functionality of our service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We use trusted third-party services for hosting and infrastructure:

  • NeonDB — PostgreSQL database hosting
  • Cloudinary — File storage for PDFs and media
  • Google (Gmail) — Email communication

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for essential functionality:

  • Session cookie — to maintain your session
  • CSRF cookie — for form security
  • Theme preference cookie — to remember your dark/light mode choice

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure database connections, and environment-based secrets management to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any non-essential data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
